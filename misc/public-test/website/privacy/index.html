Privacy Policy

This policy explains what information Skyrim Unbound collects, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices available to you. It applies to the website, launcher, UCP, whitelist flow, tickets, and related online services we operate.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Introduction

We collect only the information reasonably needed to operate the service, secure user accounts, process whitelist and support requests, investigate abuse, and maintain the continuity of the community. Our service is separate from Bethesda, ZeniMax, and platform-holder account systems, which remain governed by their own privacy policies.

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account registration information such as email address, username, and password credentials.
  • Recovery information such as security questions, verification preferences, and two-factor setup details.
  • Whitelist applications, character descriptions, forum-style submissions, and support ticket messages.
  • Optional community details, such as linked Discord information, forum group preferences, or supporter status information.
  • Any messages or materials you send to staff through tickets, appeals, applications, or support channels.

Information Collected Automatically

  • IP addresses, approximate connection history, browser and device information, timestamps, and session identifiers.
  • Security and login events, including verification challenges, password reset requests, and suspicious access checks.
  • Operational logs related to gameplay connectivity, witnessed chat logs, moderation actions, and anti-abuse review.
  • Basic site usage data needed to keep pages, authentication, downloads, and support tools functioning.

How We Use Personal Information

  • To create and maintain accounts, characters, and service access.
  • To process whitelist applications, tickets, moderation cases, and security checks.
  • To detect fraud, ban evasion, exploit abuse, and suspicious activity.
  • To send account-security, service, or transaction-related communications.
  • To improve service stability, investigate bugs, and maintain internal audit trails.

How We Share Information

We do not sell personal information. We may share limited data only when needed to run the service or comply with law, including with infrastructure providers, payment processors, email delivery providers, security tooling, or authorities when legally required.

If you follow a link to a third-party site such as Discord, YouTube, a payment provider, or SkyMP, that third party handles your information under its own rules and policies.

Retention

  • Account data is generally retained while your account remains active and for a reasonable period afterward for security, support, and legal purposes.
  • Security logs, audit records, and moderation records may be retained longer where needed to prevent abuse, resolve disputes, or comply with obligations.
  • Payment-related records may be retained for tax, accounting, fraud prevention, and chargeback handling.
  • Some gameplay or chat-related records may be trimmed, rotated, or deleted as part of normal operations.

Your Rights and Choices

Depending on the laws that apply to you, you may have the right to request access, correction, deletion, or restriction of certain personal information, and to ask us how your information is being used.

  • You can update certain account information through the UCP.
  • You can request account help, correction, or closure through staff support.
  • We may need to retain some records despite a deletion request where security, legal, fraud-prevention, or moderation obligations make that necessary.

Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect account and service data. That said, no online service can promise perfect security, and we cannot guarantee that unauthorized access, breaches, or data loss will never happen.

You should protect your account with a strong password, a secure email address, and two-factor authentication where available.

Children and Sensitive Content

Skyrim Unbound is not directed toward children. If local law requires parental permission for younger users, that permission must be obtained before use. We do not knowingly invite unlawful or highly sensitive personal information through the service, and you should avoid posting personal information that you do not want staff or other relevant users to see.

Changes and Contact

We may update this policy as the service changes. Material changes may be posted on the website, in the UCP, or through service notices. If you have privacy questions, please contact staff through the UCP or our public support channels.
