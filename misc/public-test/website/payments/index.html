Payments Policy

This page explains how Skyrim Unbound treats supporter payments, digital deliveries, chargebacks, and refund requests. It is meant to set clear expectations before money is spent on optional community benefits.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Introduction

Any payments made to Skyrim Unbound are for the optional digital benefits, supporter recognition, or account-linked services that we describe at the time of purchase. They do not purchase ownership of the server, of Bethesda intellectual property, or of any permanent right to uninterrupted service.

What Payments Can Cover

  • Supporter tags, donor badges, or account-linked forum/community groups.
  • Optional access to supporter conveniences or cosmetic recognition where offered.
  • Digital account-linked benefits that we clearly list on the purchase page or support material.

Payments do not buy the Skyrim game itself, any Bethesda or ZeniMax license, or any promise that the service will remain online forever.

Delivery and Activation

  • Most digital benefits are delivered to the account used during purchase or manually applied after staff review.
  • You are responsible for using the correct account and contact information when making a payment.
  • If a purchase requires manual approval, anti-fraud review, or staff verification, delivery may take additional time.

Refunds

Because these are generally digital, immediately usable, or manually applied community benefits, payments are ordinarily treated as final once delivered or activated. However, we may review refund requests where:

  • the purchased benefit was never delivered,
  • we made a clear billing error,
  • duplicate transactions occurred, or
  • applicable law requires a refund.

Nothing here removes any refund or consumer-protection rights that cannot legally be waived in the jurisdiction that applies to you.

Chargebacks and Payment Abuse

If a payment is reversed through a chargeback, dispute, or fraud claim after benefits were delivered, we may suspend or restrict the affected account while the issue is reviewed. We may also revoke supporter benefits, linked roles, or other digital items related to the disputed payment.

  • Fraudulent payment activity may lead to permanent account closure.
  • We may ask for supporting information to resolve billing disputes.

Payment Processors and Third Parties

Payments may be handled by third-party providers rather than directly by Skyrim Unbound. Those providers process card, wallet, or other billing information under their own terms and privacy policies. We recommend reviewing those policies before purchase.

Supporter Status, Discord, and Forum Perks

If your purchase is tied to supporter groups, Discord roles, or account-linked forum badges, those benefits may depend on linking the correct account and remaining in good standing. Loss of access to the related account, chargebacks, bans, or abuse may result in removal of the related perks.

No Investment or Ownership Interest

Payments to Skyrim Unbound are community support purchases, not investments. They do not create shares, equity, ownership, revenue participation, partnership rights, or any right to control the project, its staff, its infrastructure, or its future direction.

Questions and Support

If you have a payment issue, use the UCP ticket system or our support channels as soon as possible so the problem can be reviewed before a chargeback escalates the matter.
